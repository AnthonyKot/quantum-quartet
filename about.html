About this project

Method

Each chapter pairs the four authors on a single topic — the uncertainty principle, spin, perturbation theory, hydrogen — and asks how each one enters the material. What experiment do they start from? What do they take as axiom? What do they skip or defer? Where do they agree, and where does their disagreement reveal something deeper about the subject?

There are no rankings. The point is not to crown a best author but to read the differences productively — to see what each treatment illuminates that the others obscure.

Content policy

All prose on this site is original synthesis. Direct quotations from the four books are rare and kept short (under fifteen words) with attribution. Derivations, proofs, and figures are not reproduced.

Reference verification

Every "read the originals" pointer is verified against the edition listed above. Where a reference has not yet been checked against the source, it is visibly marked unverified on the page — never silently guessed.
